We are a new business that has just been set up in the Philippines. We sell different varieties of rice. Our goal is to make every type of rice available to the online market.



At the moment we sell:



Basmati rice


Its name means "queen of fragrance." Aromatic rice from India, Pakistan and Iran, it is known for its nutty aroma and taste.



Arborio rice


Medium-grain, cultivated in northern Italy, creamy outside and firm inside. It's used mainly in risotto; similar to a California short-grain variety called pearl.



Black rice


Rice grown in Indonesia and the Philippines, with a long black grain and nutty flavor. It's often used in puddings and cakes.



Brown rice


Unmilled rice with hull removed. The rice comes in various shades of brown, red or black; available in short, medium or long grain.



Jasmine rice


Unmilled rice with hull removed. The rice comes in various shades of brown, red or black; available in short, medium or long grain.
